Before you write

A few quick answers

How fast do you actually reply?

Almost always within a few hours during business hours, and never longer than one business day. We're small, so your message reaches a human directly.

What should I have ready?

Nothing. If you have your current site URL or a rough sense of what's not working, great — but we can figure all of that out together on the call.

Do you work outside Ontario?

Yes. We're fully remote and work with small businesses across Canada and the US. Time zones, video calls, shared docs — everything happens online.

Will I get a sales pitch?

No. The call is genuinely free, and if we're not the right fit we'll say so and point you somewhere that might be. We'd rather pass than oversell.
