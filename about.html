Mission & Vision

Why We Do What We Do.

We believe every small business owner deserves access to the same quality of digital media as the largest brands — without sacrificing their budget or their time.


Omanyi Media was born out of frustration. Our founders watched too many talented, hardworking small business owners lose customers to bigger competitors — not because their product was inferior, but because their digital presence was. We built Omanyi Media to change that.


Today we serve small business owners across Canada and the United States, delivering websites and video content that are built with strategy at their core — not just aesthetics.

To empower small businesses with professional digital media — websites and video content — that drive real growth, build lasting trust, and level the playing field.
— Our Mission, Omanyi Media

Our Journey

Five Years of Growth.

From a two-person startup to a full-service digital media company — here is how we got here.



Join Our Client List →
2021
Omanyi Media Founded

Jordan launched Omanyi Media in Toronto with a laptop, a camera, and a conviction that small businesses deserved better digital support. First three client websites launched in Year 1.

2022
Video Production Division Launched

Responded to growing client demand by adding full video production services. Completed 20+ video projects in the first year, including brand videos and social media content series.

2023
Expanded to US Clients

Launched remote service delivery to serve US-based small businesses. Team grew to five full-time members. Priya Nair joined as Head of Web Development.

2024
30-Day Website Delivery Guarantee

Launched our signature Small Business Website Package with an industry-leading 30-day delivery guarantee — a first in the Canadian small-business web design space.

2025
100th Client Milestone

Celebrated serving our 100th small business client. Recognized by the Toronto Business Association for outstanding service to the local small business community.
