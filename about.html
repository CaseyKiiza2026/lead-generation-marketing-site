About Omanyi.Media

A small studio building websites that earn their place on your homepage.

We're based in Ontario, Canada. We work remotely with small businesses across Canada and the US. We don't have a fancy office, a sales floor, or a corporate parent — and that's the point.

Why we exist

Small businesses deserve better than what they've been sold.

The way most small businesses get a website goes one of two ways. Path one: they pay an agency $10,000 for a slow, over-engineered site that takes nine months and still doesn't generate leads. Path two: they try a drag-and-drop builder, end up with something that looks like 50,000 other businesses, and quietly lose customers to competitors who took it more seriously.

We started Omanyi.Media because there's a missing middle. Small businesses need sites that are professionally designed, built to rank, and actually convert — without the price tag or runaround of an agency, and without the templated mediocrity of a builder.

We're an early-stage studio. That means you'll work directly with the people doing the work. No account manager, no junior designer, no handoffs to a team three time zones away. You'll know exactly who's building your site and exactly what it'll cost — before you hand over a dollar.

"A website is the single piece of marketing every customer touches before they decide to call. If it's bad, you're paying for that every day in lost business." — Our working principle
What we believe

Four convictions that shape every project.

01

A site is only as good as the calls it generates.

Pretty is not enough. If your site doesn't move visitors toward booking, calling, or buying — it's decoration. We measure success in inquiries, not impressions.

02

Honest beats clever.

No fake testimonials, no inflated metrics, no "trusted by 500+ companies" when the number is six. We'd rather earn your trust slowly than lose it with one cheap claim.

03

Speed is a feature.

A site that loads in five seconds is a site nobody waits for. We hand-build every page for performance — no bloated builders, no fifty plugins, no quietly degraded mobile experience.

04

You should own everything.

Your domain. Your hosting. Your content. Your analytics. We don't hold any of it hostage. If we ever stopped working together, you'd walk away with full control of your site.

Who we are

Two people who build the websites — and answer the calls.

We're two computer science graduates who build conversion-focused websites for small businesses. When you work with us, you work directly with the people doing the work — no account managers, no handoffs.

We keep our projects focused and our communication direct on purpose. It's how the work stays sharp and the timelines stay honest.
